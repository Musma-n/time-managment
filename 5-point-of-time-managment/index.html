Time managment tips

  1. Make a To-Do List: Write down everything you need to do. This helps you stay organized and not forget anything important.
  2. Prioritize Tasks: Focus on the most important tasks first. Do what matters most before moving on to other things.
  3. Set Time Limits: Give yourself a set amount of time to complete each task. This keeps you focused and prevents you from spending too much time on one thing.
  4. Avoid Multitasking: Try to focus on one task at a time. Doing too many things at once can make you less productive.
